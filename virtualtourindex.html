TIP 🤔:FINDING YOUR CLASSROOM

For Example, lets look for the class 2B1.

  1. '2', is the building number. All faculty and school buildings are numbered. These numbers are found on the outside of each building. For example, the building number for The School of Computing and Information Technology is Two (2).
  2. 'B', is the floor location. Each floor is classified alphabetically in ascending order. For example, the first floor would be 'A' and the second floor 'B'.
  3. '1', is the classroom's number. So if there are 15 classes on floor B. They are numbered from 1-15. Example, 2B1, 2B2, 2B3, etc.
